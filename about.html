About

Meet Jana

The coach behind English Mastered — and why she gets results with Arabic speakers other tutors miss.

I’ve been an English teacher for over five years. I’ve taught in secondary schools, universities, and for several NGOs in Lebanon and Turkey. I founded English Mastered in 2021 because I kept meeting the same person: an Arabic speaker who'd studied English for years and still froze the moment they had to use it.

I know that freeze. Arabic is my first language, and I learned English exactly the way my students do. I know the sound your mouth defaults to, the word order your brain reaches for, and that rule that “should” work but doesn’t. That’s what years of textbooks can’t give you — and it’s the whole reason English Mastered exists.

I studied English Language and Literature at Lebanese University, where I also earned my Teaching Diploma. I attended the TESOL 2024 conference in Tampa, Florida, and participated in Eurasia Foundation's English Language Exchange for Lebanon, a professional development exchange in the U.S. for Lebanese English teachers. I also completed online course design training at Arizona State University.
