Fee-free ways to pay

These methods skip the processing fees entirely — which is why they get the discounted price. Just send the discounted amount for your package ($15, $65, or $115) and message Jana to confirm.

Pay with USDT

Stablecoin · discounted price

  1. Message Jana on WhatsApp and she'll send the wallet address.
  2. Send the discounted amount for your package — $15, $65, or $115 — in USDT on the TRC-20 (Tron) network only.
  3. Share the transaction link or screenshot to confirm.

Only send USDT on the TRC-20 network, and double-check the address before sending — crypto transfers can't be reversed.

Whish · Western Union · OMT

Cash & wallet transfer · discounted price

  1. Message Jana on WhatsApp and tell her which package you'd like.
  2. She'll send the exact recipient details for Whish, Western Union, or OMT.
  3. Make the transfer and share your reference number (the MTCN for Western Union).
  4. She confirms it's received and books your first lesson.

Sending the details over WhatsApp keeps your transfer secure and makes sure the recipient name matches exactly.
